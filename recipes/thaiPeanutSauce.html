Thai Peanut Sauce

A delicious looking dish of Thai Peanut Sauce

This is my favourite dish to order from my favourite Thai place. The dish is Thai Peanut Sauce, sometimes called Pad Peanut Sauce, and my favourite restaurant is Vientiane, on Marion Street.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broccoli
  • Mushrooms
  • Celery
  • Snow Peas
  • Peanut Sauce

Steps

  1. Fry up some pieces of chicken breast. Thinly sliced.
  2. Add vegetables to wok when chicken is almost done cooking. Cook until starting to brown.
  3. Add peanut sauce to wok and serve over rice.
  4. Once you've screwed it all up, place an order of Pad Peanut Sauce with Vientiane.
